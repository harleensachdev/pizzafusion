Guide

  • HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys or swipe in the direction you want to move (tablet/phone) to move the tiles.
  • When water and flour touch, they get promoted to dough
  • When water and tomato touch, they get promoted to sauce
  • When dough and sauce touch, they get promoted to pizza
  • When pizza and toppings (cheese, pepperoni, ham, pineapple, mushrooms) touch, they get promoted
  • When Pizza cheese and pepperoni and others touch, they get promoted to Pizza cheese pepperoni
